Biblically Accurate Children's Books
  • The Jesus Storybook Bible
    by Sally Lloyd-Jones

    This beautifully illustrated book introduces children to the narrative of the Bible, showing how every story whispers Jesus' name.

  • God Gave Us You
    by Lisa Tawn Bergren

    Celebrate the special bond between parent and child in this tender story. A perfect bedtime read for children aged 2-6, reminding them they are a precious gift from God.

  • The Biggest Story ABC
    by Kevin DeYoung

    A colorful and creative book teaching kids the Bible's story through the alphabet. Each letter represents a key event in God's grand narrative, accompanied by vibrant illustrations and playful rhymes.

  • Found: Psalm 23
    by Sally Lloyd-Jones

    A comforting retelling of Psalm 23, this book brings peace and joy to children as they learn about God’s care and love.

  • Indescribable
    by Louie Giglio

    A devotional bridging faith and science, inspiring awe in kids about God’s creation. Perfect for curious young minds.

  • Love Letters from God
    by Glenys Nellist

    This unique book includes a love letter from God on each page, reminding children of His deep and personal love for them.

  • Thoughts to Make Your Heart Sing
    by Sally Lloyd-Jones

    Short and powerful devotions for kids, written in a way to inspire them about God's love and presence in their lives.

  • The Garden, the Curtain, and the Cross
    by Carl Laferton

    This book retells the Gospel in a way that is simple and accessible for young children, focusing on how Jesus’ work on the cross changes everything.

  • The Biggest Story
    by Kevin DeYoung

    An engaging retelling of the overarching story of the Bible, showing kids how all of Scripture points to Jesus and God’s grand redemption plan.

  • The Story of God's Love for You
    by Sally Lloyd-Jones

    A beautifully written narrative that traces God’s love throughout the Bible, designed for children to understand His plan of redemption and grace.
